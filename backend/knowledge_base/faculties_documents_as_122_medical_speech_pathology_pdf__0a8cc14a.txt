[قسم] Course Description
This course focuses on understanding the general disabling conditions of infants and young children. The course provides education to the students of the clinical picture of developmental disabilities. The course also provides major emphasis of childhood disorders and the most common acquired disorders of adulthood. Presentation of the diagnostic and therapeutic guidelines of the developmental and acquired health disabilities will be acknowledged.
