[قسم] Course Description

[قسم] This course is intended to provide basic anatomic and physiologic information

[قسم] concerning the respiratory, laryngeal, and supraglottal airway as it pertains to
speech production. Particular emphasis will be devoted to the skeletal and muscular

[قسم] components of the structures involved and their movements. The speech mechanism
will be treated as a biologic system and linguistic factors will not be covered extensively in

[قسم] this course. The course is also to generate practical interest in applying this
information to clinical populations.

[قسم] Course Objectives

[قسم] Percentage

[قسم] 1. Full understanding of different human elementary tissues and detailed
structure of skeletal and muscular system.

[قسم] 15%

[قسم] 2. Full understanding of the detailed structure of the respiratory system and
function in relation to speech production.
3. Full understanding of the detailed structure of the larynx and its function for speech production.

[قسم] 4. Full understanding of the detailed structure of the oropharyngeal and nasal
passages and their function for speech production and swallowing.

[قسم] 5. Being familiar with neuroanatomy and physiology pertaining to speech
