[قسم] Course Description
This course provides an introduction to the communication disorders. By the end of this course, the student should be able to identify the development of normal communication in humans; be able to recognize and categorize any disorders that might interrupt normal communication both in production or reception; and to analyze the possible effects of these disorders on the overall day to day life of the affected person and the

[قسم] society in general
