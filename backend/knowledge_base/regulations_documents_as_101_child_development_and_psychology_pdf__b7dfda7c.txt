[قسم] Course Description
This course focuses on the basic developmental milestones of children from age 0 up to 5-years old. Emphasis will be applied to the language and hearing development.

[قسم] The course will focus on the most common developmental disorders in term of
definition, diagnosis, and suggested treatment. Adolescence period and its related problems will be discussed briefly by the end of the course.

[قسم] Course Objectives

[قسم] Percentage

[قسم] 1. To teach the student general concepts of cleft lip and palate, its
definition, types and etiology, the treatment techniques.

[قسم] 2. Teaching student detailed phonetic transcription techniques for
articulation skills and phonemic competency.

[قسم] 3. To each student the instruments assessment to verify and specify

[قسم] clinical judgment and specify required physical or functional management
needs.

[قسم] 4. Teach student how to use nasometer in both assessment and
remediation of velopharyngeal insufficiency.

[قسم] 5. Teaching the student by techniques and methods of speech and voice
therapy appropriate for work with children and adult.

[قسم] 6. Teaching the students the techniques of feeding for each cleft palate
classification, and identifying dental issues and its intervention strategies.

[قسم] 7. Teaching methods of preventing delayed language and compensatory
articulation habits.

[قسم] 8. Reviewing the surgical approaches to treat the deformity of lip, and
palate, and how it participate in improving speech.
